<objective>
## Goal
Rename the Projects panel from "Active Projects" → "Projects" (since the panel actually shows all projects across statuses, not just active ones) and add a clear visible heading to the new-project form so users immediately understand that section is for adding a new project. Resolves feedback `1448576c`.

## Purpose
The "Active Projects" label is misleading — the panel shows Active, OnHold, and Completed projects in grouped sections. Calling it "Active Projects" sets a wrong expectation and forces users to scan to discover the panel includes more than active. Separately, the project-creation form is a flat row of inputs with no visible heading; the "Add project" submit button is the only signal that the row is for creating new projects, which is easy to miss when the panel is busy.

## Output
- "Projects" replaces "Active Projects" as the panel's tab label and header
- A visible heading (e.g., `<h3>Add a new project</h3>`) precedes the new-project form, making its purpose obvious at a glance
- No changes to the in-panel "Active / OnHold / Completed" group labels (those are status filters, not the panel title)
</objective>
